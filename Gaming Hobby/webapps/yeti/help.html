INSTRUCTION MANUAL

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Press 'New Game' to begin
  2. Use the arrow keys or number pad to move around in 8-directions
  3. Move into objects to interact with them (open, pickup, attack etc.)
  4. Find the exit

GAME PLAY

Objects you may encounter:

@ you

# wall

+ door

~ water

< entrance

> exit

? scroll

! potion

$ gold

A-Z 26 different types of monsters
